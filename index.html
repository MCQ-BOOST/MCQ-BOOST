Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some quick answers to common questions about MCQ Boost. Need more help? Feel free to contact us!

Is MCQ Boost free to use?

Absolutely! The Free Plan gives you plenty of content to get started. You can opt for Premium or Enterprise to access advanced features.

How do I get certified?

Unlock our specialized certification paths by subscribing to Premium or above. Complete all required quizzes and chapters, and get an industry-recognized certificate.

Is there a mobile version?

Yes! MCQ Boost is designed primarily for mobile devices, so you can learn and practice quizzes on-the-go. Check your app store for the latest version.
