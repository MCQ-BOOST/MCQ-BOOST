Privacy Policy for MCQ Boost for Academic Study

Effective Date: 29/12/2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to MCQ Boost for Academic Study! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

MCQ Boost for Academic Study is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information ("PII") or sensitive data from our users. Specifically:

  • We do not track your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal data.
  • We do not store or process user behavior data outside the functionalities described below.
3. Features of the App

Our App provides the following features:

  • AI-Generated Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs): The App generates MCQs using Google Gemini’s generative AI capabilities.
  • In-App Purchases: Users can access premium features through in-app subscriptions or one-time purchases.
  • Database Reset: Users have the ability to reset the application database at any time.
  • Purchase Reset: Users can reset their purchase history as needed.
  • Feedback Submission: Users can send feedback via email directly through the App.
4. Subscription and In-App Purchases
  • In-App Subscriptions: The App offers subscription-based access to premium features. Details of subscription plans are available within the App. Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled in accordance with platform policies (Google Play Store or Apple App Store).
  • One-Time Purchases: Some features may be unlocked via one-time payments.

Users are responsible for managing their subscriptions and purchases through the respective app store platforms. Refunds and cancellations are subject to the policies of the app stores.

5. Use of AI Technology

The App leverages Google Gemini’s generative AI to create customized MCQs. All AI processes occur securely and do not involve the transmission of any user data to external servers.

6. Email Feedback

Users may voluntarily submit feedback via email through the App. While this feedback may include personal information (e.g., your email address), such data is only used for responding to your inquiries and improving our services. We do not store or share your feedback outside the purpose of direct communication.

For feedback, contact us at: slitidev@icloud.com.

7. Data Security

Although the App does not collect personal information, we implement industry-standard measures to ensure the security and integrity of the application and its features.

  • Database Security: The database reset functionality allows users to clear locally stored application data securely.
  • Purchase History Reset: Resetting purchases does not transmit any data to our servers.
8. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party services for its core functionalities, including:

  • Google Gemini AI: Used for generating MCQs.
  • App Stores (Google Play Store, Apple App Store): For subscription management and in-app purchases.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their respective privacy policies for more information.

9. Disclaimer

The content generated by the App, including MCQs, is for educational purposes only. While we strive for accuracy, we do not guarantee the correctness or applicability of the generated content for specific purposes.

The App and its services are provided "as is" without warranties of any kind. By using the App, you agree that we are not liable for any direct, indirect, or consequential damages arising from its use.

10. User Rights

As we do not collect user data, your interaction with the App remains entirely anonymous. Should you choose to submit feedback, you may request the deletion of such communications by contacting us via the provided support email.

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: slitidev@icloud.com

Thank you for using MCQ Boost for Academic Study! Your privacy and security are our top priorities.
